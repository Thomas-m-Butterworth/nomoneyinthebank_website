The Caramel is Salted With My Tears | Zine

The Caramel is Salted with my Tears is a zine of vegan baking recipes, put together by comedian Thom Bee during his time not allowed out of his parent's house after a mental health collapse. Everything is relatively simple to make and ingredients are easy to find/already in your pantry. Recipes included; Chocolate Ginger Truffles, Chelsea Buns, Plaited Olive Bread, Spiced Pear Jam, and Lemon Meringue Tart.

This zine is a pay what you want to download, but all profit will be donated to Black Minds Matter.
