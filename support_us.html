Support Us

It isn't cheap being DIY comedy superstars, there's a lot of printing costs, web-hosting, megabuses, and bottles of wine from the train station to be taken in account.

If you like what we do, whether it's online chaos, printed zines, or anarchic live comedy, this is your place to show that. There's PayPal for one off donations, Ko-Fi if you just want to sub us a brew for our writing sessions, and Patreon if you want to chuck us some monthly dollar for heaps of exclusive and see-it-first contnent. You can even buy some merch if our logo on your stuff is your thing!

Obviously only commit within your means, life is hard, we're just happy that you're here.
