Balthazar Dark Present: Wrestling With Saddos

Balthazar Dark is the greatest wrestler you’ve never heard of. Too dangerous to perform on the scene in this country he whiles away his time exposing the seedy underbelly of pro wrestling to UK comedy audiences. Visiting whichever city he’s in to schmooze his way onto an indie bill.

Expanding on his story told in the 2018 (No) Money in the Bank Show, Balthazr aims to comprehensively explain his entire history in wrestling. An inspiring story of a wanabee, to the king of the ring, to a guy stuck in the UK peddling his story as comedy in the hope he can get back in the squared circle again one day.

Unpredicatable, wild, and with enough wrestling stories to last ten more hours, no wrestling fan can afford to miss ‘Wrestling with Saddos’.
